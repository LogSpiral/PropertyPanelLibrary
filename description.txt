属性面板是SilkyUIFramework的一个子UI库
最主要的控件是PropertyPanel
旨在对于一个对象根据它的元数据生成相应的配置界面
简化配置界面的制作与排版等

面板本身可以根据需求
自行实现相应的填充器、筛选器、排序器、装饰器四大部分

而对于面板内的控件可以根据这些控件统一的需求
自行实现相应的交互判定器、交互器、写入器、选项装饰器四大部分


Property Panel is a sub-library of SilkyUIFramework
The core component is PropertyPanel
which can generate a config panel for a object via its meta data

You can make the Filler, the Filter, 
the Sorter and the Decorator of the Panel depending on your need.

You can make the InteractableHandler, the MouseHandler, 
the Writer and the OptionDecorator of the Panel's options depending on your need as well
